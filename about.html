About Us

Bhimrao Ambedkar University

Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar University, Agra, situated in the historic city of Agra, is a prominent higher education institution in India. Established in 1927, it is named after Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar, the architect of the Indian Constitution. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. Known for its academic excellence and research initiatives, it continues to be a leading center for learning and innovation in the region, contributing significantly to the educational landscape of India.
